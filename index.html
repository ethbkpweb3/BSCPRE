About Zon

Zon is a digital token with global reach, powering multiple utilities with one token.
Profit-Sharing: The Profits generated by ZON and its ecosystem Dapps flow back to the ZON holders.
Versatile Utility: Supports governance, staking, and ecosystem service access, making it a multifaceted token.
Cross-Chain Compatibility: Seamlessly moves across different blockchains, enhancing usability and accessibility.
Deflationary Model: Limited supply ensures stability and potential value appreciation over time.
Integrated Ecosystem: Embedded in top DApps like Xdsea NFT marketplace, Roro wallet, and Zebraswap DEX, with profits going back to ZON holders.
